Z900

The Kawasaki Z900 is a high-performance motorcycle that belongs to the popular Z series produced by Kawasaki Motors. Known for its sporty design and powerful performance, the Z900 is a naked bike that combines style with cutting-edge technology.

Key features of the Kawasaki Z900 include:

1.Engine Performance: The Z900 is equipped with a liquid-cooled, four-stroke, 948cc inline-four engine, delivering impressive power and torque. The engine's design focuses on providing a thrilling riding experience and quick acceleration.

2.Chassis and Suspension: The motorcycle features a lightweight trellis frame that contributes to agile handling and responsiveness. The suspension system includes upside-down front forks and a horizontal back-link rear suspension, enhancing stability and comfort during various riding conditions.

3.Braking System: The Z900 is equipped with advanced braking components, including dual disc brakes in the front and a single disc brake at the rear. This ensures effective stopping power and control, contributing to the overall safety of the rider.

4.Design Aesthetics: With its aggressive and muscular design, the Z900 has a distinctive appearance. The exposed trellis frame, sharp lines, and minimalistic bodywork give the bike a modern and street-ready look.

5.Technology and Electronics: The Z900 incorporates modern technology features, including a full digital LCD instrument panel that provides essential information to the rider. Electronic aids such as multiple riding modes and advanced ABS contribute to a safer and more customizable riding experience.

6.Naked Bike Concept: As a naked bike, the Z900 showcases the beauty of its engineering with minimalistic fairings and an exposed engine. This design choice not only contributes to the bike's aesthetics but also emphasizes the raw, exhilarating nature of the riding experience.

The Kawasaki Z900 boasts a formidable liquid-cooled, 4-stroke, 4-cylinder DOHC engine with a displacement of 948 cc, characterized by a bore and stroke of 73.4 mm x 56.0 mm. Its compression ratio stands at 11.8:1, contributing to the engine's robust and dynamic performance. Fueled by a sophisticated fuel injection system with 36 mm Keihin throttle bodies and ignited digitally, the Z900 ensures optimal efficiency and responsiveness. This powerhouse is effortlessly started via an electric starting system, making the motorcycle a thrilling and accessible ride for enthusiasts. As with any technical specifications, it's advisable to consult the latest information from Kawasaki for the most accurate and up-to-date details.
